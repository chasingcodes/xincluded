✨ About Xincluded

🏃‍♀️ Our Mission

Welcome to Xincluded — a community-driven directory of races and running events that explicitly welcome and include non-binary participants! 🏳️‍⚧️


We believe everyone deserves the chance to participate in the sport they love, free from barriers or exclusion based on gender identity. Our platform helps runners find races with non-binary registration options, making the running community more inclusive, welcoming, and celebratory of everyone.


✨ Why I Started This

I’m Chase (they/he) — a non-binary triathlete who casually (and sometimes chaotically) does triathlons. Over the years, I’ve seen more races offer non-binary registration, which is amazing — but simply having the category doesn’t always mean the experience is inclusive.


That’s why I created Xincluded: a space powered by real feedback from real athletes. Here, we go beyond just listing races. You’ll find details like whether non-binary participants can podium and win prizes, if you can register with your chosen name, whether pronouns are acknowledged, if gender-neutral bathrooms are available, and much more.


💪 Our Approach

This isn’t just a database — it’s a growing, living resource shaped by the community. By sharing your experiences, you help other athletes feel seen, prepared, and welcomed. Together, we can keep pushing for a world where all athletes feel like they belong — on the start line, on the course, and at the finish. 🌈

Join our mission!

Help us grow this community by suggesting races and events that embrace inclusivity. Together, we can make running more welcoming for everyone! 🌈
